EMPOWER YOURSELF, BE AN ACTIVE PART OF THE LGTBI+ COMMUNITY

BeSafe allows you to report cases of violence against the community, report and receive legal or psychological advice

We believe that the visibility of problems gives us tools for a change, we want that the information to give you tools to be able to act and at the same time that you are part of all this, take care of yourself and others.

Get started

Check the map

You can consult the map with the information saved and created by users to know which sites are safe in the city and at what time without being registered. The map has filters that allow you to see the information according to the type of violence and communes in the city, which helps you to process the information in a more specific way.

Register or login easily!

Register and log in quickly, we do not request information that compromises you and if you wish you can do it through your Google account

Report, inform the community!

When you are registered, you can report any type of violence against you or any member of the community. We have designed a brief and friendly interface to be able to load the information quickly and accurately, if you wish, the application will take the location where you are automatically.

Start now

What are you waiting for, the community is waiting for you! We want you to be part of all this, we do not want you to be silent or alone, it is important to visualize the problem to achieve changes.

Why "BeSafe"?

We celebrate the dierence, meeting in this space makes us grow not only professionally but also as people. This meeting, this environment, was conducive to empathize with the other, see the position of the other, respect the other, help the other ... specifically, create and feel in a safe environment. And what is a safe environment? An environment where one can be free from prejudice. This is how BeSafe was born, from empathy. In our group there are members who define themselves as Queer, Trans, Cis, we are a diverse group and we firmly believe that technology can change the world, that information empowers us and provides us with tools for change. BeSafe seeks to create a space where the LGTBI + community can record acts of violence and use this data not only for personal use but also to create a database with real statistics on the problem of violence in the community.
